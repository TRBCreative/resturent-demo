Years of Legacy

Our Heritage

A Legacy of Culinary Brilliance

Founded in 1996 by visionary Chef Jean-Pierre Moreau, Élysée was born from a dream — to create a dining sanctuary where French tradition meets modern artistry. What began as a 20-seat intimate bistro has evolved into one of New York's most celebrated culinary destinations.

"Cooking is not just about feeding the body — it is about nourishing the soul, one plate at a time."

Every ingredient is handpicked from local farms and artisan purveyors. Every dish is composed with the precision of a jeweler and the passion of a painter. Every guest is treated as family.

JP

Jean-Pierre Moreau

Founder & Executive Chef
