Our Story

A Legacy of Culinary Excellence

Founded in 1996 by Chef Antoine Laurent, La Maison has been a cornerstone of fine dining in New York City. What began as a small French bistro has evolved into an award-winning restaurant celebrated for its innovative cuisine and impeccable service.

Every dish at La Maison is crafted with the finest seasonal ingredients sourced from local farms and artisan producers. Our menu is a harmonious blend of classic French techniques and contemporary culinary innovation, designed to delight all your senses.

We believe dining is not just about food — it's about creating memories, celebrating moments, and experiencing the art of hospitality at its finest.

AL

Antoine Laurent

Founder & Head Chef
